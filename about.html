My story

Francisco Javier Guardado is a person with great values and respect for others. He comes from Houston, Texas. He lived in El Salvador during his teen years where he went to Nursing School and helped people from all over the country at health care stablishments and public hospitals.

Youtube channel Belzarion was born in 2019. After the creator of this channel realized his passion in Computer Science, he discovered joy for video games. Francisco also developed an interactive devotion to learn how video games are designed and worked.

Wisdom, strength, and courage. If you missed it, these three concepts come from a video game, but also useful in life.

Belzarion and The Zurfaro are Youtube channels that take away stress from your shoulders. Imagine learning without never getting bored. Oh and don't forget the background music. They are cool.
